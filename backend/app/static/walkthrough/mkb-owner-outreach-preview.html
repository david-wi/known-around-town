The Outreach Approach

The message introduces the directory, explains that the salon is already listed, and invites them to claim their listing. The first 25 claimers get Founding Partner status — that's the scarcity hook. Each message is personalized with the salon name and a direct link to their listing.

Three channels:

  • Email — for the 3 salons with direct addresses
  • Instagram DM — for the 7 without email (they're all active on Instagram)
  • Phone call — as a backup for anyone who doesn't respond

To be sent from David or hello@knowsbeauty.com. Personalize [Salon Name] and [Claim URL] per row in the table below.

Subject: [Salon Name] is on Miami Knows Beauty
Hi [Owner's name or "team"], We just launched Miami Knows Beauty — a neighborhood-by-neighborhood guide to the best salons, spas, and beauty studios in South Florida. [Salon Name] is already listed. Claiming takes about two minutes. No credit card needed to start: [Claim URL — e.g. miami.knowsbeauty.com/owners?slug=wynwood-hair-co] Happy to answer any questions. David Bodnick Miami Knows Beauty
